Magic Boxes in Dart: Boxes with Tool Bar

Canvas is not supported in your browser.

Creating and Moving Boxes

Click on the board to create a box. Cklick on the box to select it. Click again to deselect the box. To move a non selected box, click on the box and keep the mouse down while moving it. A selected box cannot be moved.

Tool Bar Problem

When both button and canvas events are active, there is a conflict that I do not understand. Uncomment both on (button) click events in the ToolBar class to get the problem: boxes cannot be created. The code is kept at GitHub.
